    Jerzy BUZEK, EP President, visits to Libya: Meeting with civil society and visit to the Susha and Al-Hayat refugee camps on the Libyan/Tunisian border
    Current language VI Available in VI
    Classifications BUZEK, Jerzy Karol (EPP, PL) • Libya
    Event Date 30/10/2011
    Duration 05:40
    Reference 80394
    Background "You have won the struggle over Gaddafi. Now you have to win the peace and your future," EP President Jerzy Buzek said during a visit to Libya. "I wish that all Libyans - men and women - reap the harvest of their fight for human dignity and freedom. The new Libya must be a Libya of peace and justice, of democracy and law." During a two-day visit Mr Buzek met Mustafa Abdul Jalil, the chair of the transitional council, members of civil society. He also talked to the public in Martyrs' Square and visited the Susha and Al-Hayat refugee camps on the Libyan/Tunisian border.
